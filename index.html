BayesComp-ISBA. The Bayesian Computation Section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis.

BayesComp-ISBA is a section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis (ISBA). The purposes of the Section are as multifaceted as the aspects of Bayesian computation, including

  1. Promoting original research into computational methods for inference and decision making.
  2. Encouraging the use of frontier computational tools among practitioners.
  3. Encouraging the development of adapted software, languages, platforms, and dedicated machines.
  4. Translating and disseminating among statisticians methods developed in other disciplines.

Join BayesComp-ISBA

Are you interested in Bayesian computation? Would you like to participate in activities to promote your research and learn about the latest research in Bayesian computation? Join BayesComp-ISBA!

We welcome researchers at any level. To become a BayesComp-ISBA member, subscribe to ISBA here. In the memberships form you will have the possibility to add your one-year membership to BayesComp-ISBA (Bayesian Computation Section) for only $5 more, or even a lifetime membership for $75.
