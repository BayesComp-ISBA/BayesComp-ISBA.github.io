BayesComp-ISBA organizes the biennial Bayes Comp world meeting, sessions in other meetings like ISBA and JSM and endorses other workshops, short courses and events.

Bayes Comp is the current incarnation of the popular MCMSki series of conferences. Bayes Comp 2020 in Gainesville, Florida was the second edition of this conference series and Bayes Comp 2018 in Barcelona, Spain was the first edition.

We are excited to announce that we are beginning a series of online meetings, starting with an event on Measuring the Quality of MCMC Output in October, 2021. More details will appear here.
