BayesComp-ISBA events

BayesComp-ISBA organizes the biennial Bayes Comp world meeting (formerly "MCMSki"), sessions in other meetings like ISBA and JSM and endorses other workshops, short courses and events.

Here's a list of future and past events.

  • Bayes Comp 2027, Texas A&M University, US
  • Bayes Comp 2025, National University of Singapore, Singapore
  • Bayes Comp 2023 15-17 March 2023 in Levi, Finland .
  • Measuring the Quality of MCMC Output, an online workshop held in October, 2021, see more details on the website.
  • Bayes Comp 2020 in Gainesville, Florida.
  • Bayes Comp 2018 in Barcelona, Spain.
  • MCMSki 5 2016 in Lenzerheide, Switzerland.
  • MCMSki 4 2014. in Chamonix, France.
  • MCMSki 3 2011. in Utah, USA.
  • MCMSki 2 2008. in Bormio, Italy.
  • MCMSki 1 2005, in Bormio, Italy. The conference website is now down, but the website of the Adap'ski satellite meeting is still up.
